The article outlines six key trends Systems Integrators need to adopt for data migrations: structured validation processes, regulatory integration into pipelines, shift-left testing with data contracts, architecture for portability with open formats, automation of expertise through playbooks, and designing migrations with zero downtime and rollback capabilities.
