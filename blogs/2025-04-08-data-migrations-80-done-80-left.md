The article explains why data migrations often stall in their final stages, identifying two bottlenecks: complex models that are difficult to translate and the challenge of validating that new systems function identically to legacy ones. Proper tooling and feedback loops, such as Datafold's Migration Agent, help teams move from almost done to genuinely complete.
