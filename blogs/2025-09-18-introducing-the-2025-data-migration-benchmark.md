Datafold introduces its 2025 Data Migration Benchmark Survey, aimed at gathering insights from practitioners about migration timelines, budgets, validation methods, and AI adoption. The survey seeks reliable data on how long code migrations take and what they cost so teams can compare their experiences to industry standards.
