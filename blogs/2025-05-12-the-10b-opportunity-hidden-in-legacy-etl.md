Legacy ETL systems represent a significant business opportunity for data platform migration partners. Outdated ETL pipelines built with GUI-based tools from the 1990s-2000s create bottlenecks; partners equipped with automated migration and validation tools can differentiate themselves by safely tackling these complex transformations.
