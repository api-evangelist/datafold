Datafold has joined the Databricks Delivery Provider Program, an invitation-only partnership enabling fixed-price, guaranteed-timeline migrations to Databricks with proven data quality assurance. Customers can purchase migrations directly from Databricks while leveraging Datafold's AI-first approach to modernize legacy data infrastructure in weeks rather than years.
