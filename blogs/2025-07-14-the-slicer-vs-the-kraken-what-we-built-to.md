Datafold developed the "Slicer" technology to handle the migration of an exceptionally large 300,000-character stored procedure. The Slicer analyzes code structure to identify logical boundaries and preserve dependencies, enabling translation into modular dbt models while maintaining functional integrity where conventional AI tools fail due to context window limits.
